Paul Willian Walker IV was an american actor, social activist and philanthropist. He was best know for his role as Brian O'conner in The Fast and The Furious franchise.

Walker began his career as a child actor during the 1970 and 1980s, but first gained recognition in the early 1990s after appearing in the televison soap opera the young and the restless.
